The Gap No Syllabus Names

You were trained to pass tests. Almost no one trained you to build a life.

Formal education is excellent at transferring information and quiet about everything else — judgment, resilience, a working theory of how reality actually operates. Caravan exists for adults who've noticed that gap: people who've done well by conventional measures and still feel the floor is thinner than it should be. The curriculum won't hand you certainty. It hands you a rigorous, hands-on way to go find it yourself.

  • Real projects with a mentor and a deliverable, not lectures and a grade
  • Primary sources over secondhand summaries, in every unit
  • A community that tests ideas against direct experience, not consensus
